## Programming a Robot to Follow a Trajectory

# 🐾 Steps:
  - Move forward for 3 seconds at 1 m/s.
  - Stop for 1 second.
  - Rotate counterclockwise for 2 seconds at 1 rad/s.
  - Stop for 1 second.
  - Move backward for 3 seconds at -0.5 m/s.
  - Stop again for 1 second.
